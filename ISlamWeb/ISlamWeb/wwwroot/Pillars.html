The Five Pillars of Islam

🕌

Shahada

Faith: Declaring there is no god but Allah, and Muhammad is His messenger.

🙏

Salah

Prayer: Performing five daily prayers to connect with Allah.

🤲

Zakat

Charity: Giving to those in need to purify wealth and heart.

🌙

Sawm

Fasting: Observing fast during the holy month of Ramadan.

🕋

Hajj

Pilgrimage: Visiting the holy sites in Mecca at least once in a lifetime.
